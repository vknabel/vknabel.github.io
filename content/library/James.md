The book follows a slave in the southern USA during his escape journey. Based on _Huckleberry Finn_: a book I've never read.
Hence I read it with relatively few preconceptions. Topic-wise _James_ tackles a dark chapter of humanity, particularly of the USA, but only touches lightly on the harsh sufferings.

The ending feels a bit rushed and has a somewhat forced happy ending. It’s enjoyable to read, yet I felt the author unnecessarily held back on his criticism.
