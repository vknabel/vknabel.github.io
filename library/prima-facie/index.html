Prima Facie

Posted on 2026-06-27 – Suzie Miller, Kjona Verlag, ISBN 978-3-910372-45-0 Katharina Martl

I devoured this book. Told from the perspective of a defense attorney, it explores sexual offenses, critiques the legal system, and exposes the immense obstacles victims face in seeking justice, as well as the alarmingly high number of unreported cases.

The story is compelling and beautifully written—you feel and live every moment alongside the protagonist. The pacing is expertly controlled, with intense passages balanced by quieter, reflective ones. If you’re looking for an emotional, well-crafted book that reads effortlessly, you’ll find it here—and be delighted.
